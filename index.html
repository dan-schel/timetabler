About Timetabler

Timetabler is a tool designed to help you visualise your uni timetable preferences while you choose them.

It's a project essentially born out of frustration with the fact that Allocate+ gives you nothing but a table of options per class when considering your timetable, and seemly expects you to memorize all the previous classes' options when you move on to the next one.

This tool allows you to see all your current choices visually on one timetable grid, and drag-and-drop blocks between options until you're satisfied with your choices. You can then export everything, in case you want to fiddle with them again later.
